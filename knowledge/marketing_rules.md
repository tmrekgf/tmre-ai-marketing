# Marketing Rules & Guidelines

- Always disclose DTCP/RERA approval status in ads and promotional material
- Do not make guaranteed ROI or price-appreciation claims
- Follow local real estate advertising regulations (RERA compliance)
- Get legal/compliance review before publishing large campaigns
- Ensure all pricing shown is current and accurate
- Use approved brand voice and tone across all channels

Always include CTA.

Always mention project location.

Always mention approvals.

Keep Facebook posts below 200 words.

Instagram captions under 120 words.

Use emojis sparingly.

End with phone number.

Always use TMRE branding.
